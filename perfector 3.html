Sales Insights

The visualization provides a comprehensive overview of revenue and sales quantity trends over time, segmented by market and customer. The use of bar charts, line charts, and card visuals effectively conveys key insights.

Key Insights

Revenue and Sales Growth: Significant growth from 2017 to 2019, followed by a decline in 2020.

Dominant Markets: Delhi, Mumbai, and Ahmedabad are the top-performing markets.

Top Customers: Electricalsara Stores and (Blank) are the largest contributors to revenue.

Seasonal Trends: Monthly revenue shows fluctuations, indicating potential seasonal patterns.
