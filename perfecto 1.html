REVENUE INSIGHTS IN HOSPITALITY

The visualization provides a comprehensive overview of hotel performance metrics, including revenue, occupancy, ADR (Average Daily Rate), and DSRN (Daily Revenue per Available Room). The interactive elements and clear data presentation make it easy to understand key trends and insights.

KEY INSIGHTS:

Revenue and Occupancy: Overall, there's a positive trend in both revenue and occupancy. However, there are fluctuations across different weeks and properties.

ADR and DSRN: The ADR and DSRN metrics show variations across properties, indicating differences in pricing strategies and operational efficiency.

Booking Platform: The pie chart highlights the distribution of bookings across various platforms, with Booking.com and Agoda being the major contributors.

Room Class Performance: The bar chart shows the distribution of bookings across different room classes, with a focus on standard and deluxe rooms.

Property-Level Analysis: The table provides detailed information about individual properties, including revenue, occupancy, ADR, DSRN, and other key metrics.
